## ✨ **What's New**

### 1️⃣ **"The Nuts" Display** 👑

Now when you analyze any hand, you'll see:

**NEW SECTION: "THE NUTS"**
- Shows the **best possible hand** that can be made with the current board
- Tells you **exactly what you need** to make it
- **Highlights when YOU have it** with 👑 crown emoji
- Yellow glow effect when you have the nuts

**Example:**
```
Board: A♠ K♠ Q♠ 5♥ 2♣

THE NUTS:
Straight Flush
Need 2 cards in ♠ that make a straight

Your Hand: J♠ 10♠
✅ You have it! 👑🎉
```

**Why it's useful:**
- Know what the best possible hand is
- Understand what beats you
- See if you have the unbeatable hand
- Learn board texture reading

### 2️⃣ **Play vs Bots Mode** 🤖🎮

**BRAND NEW GAME MODE!**

Click the **"🎮 Play vs Bots"** button to play real poker against AI!

**Features:**
- ✅ Full poker game with betting rounds
- ✅ 3-7 AI opponents with personalities
- ✅ Blind structure ($10/$20)
- ✅ Starting chips ($1,000)
- ✅ Real betting actions (Fold, Check, Call, Raise)
- ✅ All streets (pre-flop, flop, turn, river)
- ✅ Pot tracking
- ✅ Chip management

**Bot Personalities:**
1. 🦈 **Sarah "The Shark"** - Aggressive player
2. 🗿 **Mike "The Rock"** - Tight player
3. 🃏 **Alex "Wild Card"** - Loose player
4. 💎 **Jamie "The Pro"** - Balanced GTO style
5. 🎭 **Sam "Bluff Master"** - Frequent bluffer
6. 🧮 **Chris "Calculator"** - Mathematical
7. 🤪 **Taylor "Maniac"** - Crazy plays
8. 🧊 **Jordan "Ice Cold"** - Calm and collected

**How to Play:**
1. Click **🎮 Play vs Bots**
2. Game starts automatically
3. See your 2 hole cards
4. Choose your action:
   - **Fold** - Give up your hand
   - **Check** - No bet (if allowed)
   - **Call** - Match current bet
   - **Raise $50** - Increase the bet
5. Watch bots play their turns
6. Community cards are dealt automatically
7. Play through all streets
8. Click **New Hand** to play again

**Current Actions:**
- Fold, Check, Call work perfectly
- Raise by $50 increments
- Bots make realistic decisions based on their style
